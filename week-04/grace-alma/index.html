MY FONDEST MEMORIES

I have been to many places, but my fondest memories are in the following:

Toronto, Ontario

I flew more than 30000 miles just to live and work here in Toronto. It is cold bu I am loving it.

Las Vegas, Nevada

My husband and I went to Las Vegas for a week. It was fun - good place, great food and good bargains. But I lost in the slot machines!

Punta Cana, Dominican Republic

A week of Honeymoon in 2010. Pure luxury! I cannot forget the fresh lobster and the hot tub in the room!

Cozumel, Mexico

Last Christmas holiday, we flew to Cozumel. Beautiful island and awesome water. Great food and friendly people. I love the place!

Boracay, Philippines

This is a paradise in my homeland. My husband spent two weeks mingling with my people. Lovely place and wonderful company.

My Dream Trips

I would like to make the following trips in the near future:

Rome, Italy

Foremost, I would like to see the Vatican and maybe meet the Pope in person. And then, we can take this incredible Rome day trip to the Amalfi Coast, and discover the dreamy coastal scenery of southern Italy.

London Bridge, London, United Kingdom

A trip to London will be very exciting. I would like to see the London Bridge and the Royal London. If there is an opportunity, I would like to live like a princess! Maybe pretend...

San Francisco, California, USA

I always dream to see the Golden Gate and the Aquarium of the Bay. My friend also mentioned Fisherman's wharf, wax museum, Ripley's Believe it or not. And then, go shopping at the Union Square and take a walk at the North Beach and Chinatown!

Seattle, Washington, USA

It will be interesting to see the Pike Market Place where vendors gleefully toss salmon to each other and crack jokes. And then visit the Seattle Center, a cultural complex where you could easily spend hours at the Pacific Science Center, Chihuly glass display, food court, theatres or simply watching kids frolic in a giant outdoor fountain. We would probably try riding the Washington State Ferry and the Space Needle! Or take a stroll in the Olympic Sculpture Park.

Paris, France

I would like to see the Eiffel Tower and enjoy dinner at the Eiffel Tower's 58 Tour Eiffel restaurant. Then see the Louvre Museum and the Notre Dame Cathedral. A visit to the home of the Cancan, the famous Moulin Rouge in bohemian Montmartre will also be great. Or, even just taking a stroll, sipping coffee and go shopping!
